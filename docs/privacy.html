Privacy Policy - YourNotes

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

YourNotes does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data whatsoever.

Data Collection

YourNotes collects zero data. This includes but is not limited to:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No device identifiers
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent externally

Internet Access

YourNotes does not require or use internet access. The app has no INTERNET permission in its Android manifest. All functionality works completely offline.

Data Storage

All your notes, labels, reminders, drawings, audio recordings, and photos are stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to any server, cloud, or external service.

Permissions Used

YourNotes requests only the minimum permissions needed for its features:

  • Camera — To take photos and attach them to notes (only when you use this feature)
  • Microphone — To record audio notes (only when you use this feature)
  • Notifications — To show reminder alerts at the time you set
  • Alarm (exact) — To schedule reminder notifications precisely

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app from your device. This permanently removes all notes, labels, reminders, and associated files.

Third-Party Services

YourNotes does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data collection tools.

Children's Privacy

YourNotes is not specifically directed at children under 13. Since the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of any age.

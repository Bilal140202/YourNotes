YourNotes — Your thoughts, completely private.

No account. No cloud. No tracking. Everything stays on your device, always.

- Rich text editing with clean, intuitive interface
- Checklists for tasks and to-dos
- Background images and color coding
- Draw and sketch freehand diagrams
- Pin important notes to the top

CAPTURE EVERYTHING

- Photo attachments — camera or gallery
- Drawing mode — annotate with freehand sketches

STAY ORGANIZED
- Labels for categorization
- Smart search by keyword, label, or type
- List and column view layouts
- Archive completed tasks

BUILT FOR PRIVACY

- No account required
- No ads, no trackers, no data collection
- Your notes never leave your device
